Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

At Jadwal (developed by Gorashe Mohamed / GriezTech), we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and basic login credentials.
  • Study Data: We store the tasks, exam schedules, and subjects you input to help you track your academic progress.
  • AI Interaction Data: When using the "AI Assistant," your prompts are sent to the Google Gemini service to generate helpful responses.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionalities.
  • To deliver personalized study tips and schedules via AI.
  • To improve app performance and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to handle data, including:

  • Supabase: For database management and secure authentication.
  • Google Gemini API: For processing AI queries.

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
jadwal.app@outlook.com
